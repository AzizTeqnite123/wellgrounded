What is grounding or earthing?

Electrically grounding the human body to the earth provides a connection to the earth’s natural supply of electrons.  With more than 20 medical studies published on the topic, it has been proven that when we are grounded, electrons flow freely into the body. And the earth’s electrons are powerful in fighting inflammation.

These electrons have a negative charge and flow into our bodies and "match up" with free radicals, which have a positive charge. When the electrons have matched up with free radicals, they are then flushed from our systems naturally. Free radicals arise through natural cycles in our bodies, aging and response to injury or illness. If free radicals do not have an available electron present, they will poach an electron from a healthy cell. This creates more damage, more free radicals, and the cycle continues.  When you connect a grounding mat or strap to a grounded outlet and put bare skin on it, your body is flooded with electrons. The biggest benefit is that inflammation in the body will begin to get processed in a way that doesn't cause further damage to healthy cells. 

Your body is grounded when you are walking barefoot on the grass outdoors or swimming in a natural source of water. Using a grounding mat or strap uses the grounding system in your house’s electrical system as a big extension cord to connect you to the earth beneath you.
